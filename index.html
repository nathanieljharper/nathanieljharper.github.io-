Summary of Events

The 38th Grand Prix du Canada took place on June 10th 2018 at the Circuit Gilles Villeneuve located in Montreal Quebec. Ferrari star Sebastian Vettel took pole position on the Saturday while the Haas of Romain Grosjean caught fire early in the qualifying session. Vettel then drove to a dominant win on Sunday and regained the lead of the World Driver's Championship from rival Lewis Hamilton.
